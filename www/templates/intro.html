What is LANDIT?

LANDIT is a location-based social platform that provides its users the freedom to enjoy and explore what a certain place has to offer. They can discover new places based from the different locations being provided which would give them a lot of ideas of things to fulfill in a bucketlist.

User rankings will also be done in accordance as to who were able to fulfill most of their bucketlist.

Live. Life. Love

Bucketlists are social. Create fun and personal location-based experiences for your family and friends. Post it in your bucketlist profile...
